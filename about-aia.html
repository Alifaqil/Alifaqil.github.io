About AIA

ABOUT AIA singapore

For almost a century, AIA has served the ever-changing needs of millions of people all around the Asia-Pacific region

For over a century, AIA has endeavoured to make a significant, positive impact for our customers and communities across Asia. Our Purpose to help millions of people live Healthier, Longer, Better Lives is at the heart of everything we do. Our Purpose guides the decisions we make and the actions we take as an organisation - empowering and enabling people to understand and manage their health, while meeting their long-term savings and protection needs.

In bringing our Purpose to life, we launched AIA One Billion (AOB) - our ambition to engage a billion people across Asia to live Healthier, Longer, Better Lives by 2030. AOB signifies our commitment to engage, educate and encourage communities to improve physical, mental and environmental wellness while championing financial inclusion. We believe that helping to create a healthier Asia is one of the most important and valuable things we can do for our communities, today and in the future.

Our passion in contributing to a healthier planet and healthier people shapes the commitment behind AIA’s Environmental, Social, and Governance (ESG) mandate to drive and deliver sustainable outcomes for the individuals and families we serve.

As a leading insurer in Singapore, we have been serving generations of Singaporeans for more than 90 years since 1931. We understand what matters most – being financially prepared for every stage in life and having good health to enjoy life with your loved ones.

The AIA team and insurance representatives are grateful to our customers for placing their trust in us. We are committed to delivering our best every day, being their trusted partner and journeying with them through their lives. We will continually enhance our capabilities to provide compelling propositions and the best service experiences to meet their evolving needs, and enable people to live Healthier, Longer, Better Lives!
